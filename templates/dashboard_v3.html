How to Interpret Your Ashtakavarga

Auspicious Houses (30+ points)

These houses are very strong and bring favorable results. Higher scores indicate more prosperity in these life areas. You can expect success, growth, and positive outcomes.

Average Houses (25-29 points)

Mixed or average results. Affairs proceed without significant gains or major troubles. These areas require moderate effort and attention.

Inauspicious Houses (Below 25 points)

Potential challenges and less favorable outcomes. These areas require more effort, attention, and strategic planning to achieve desired results.
